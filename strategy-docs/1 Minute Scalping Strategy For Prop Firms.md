# Video 2: My Exact 1 Minute Scalping Strategy For Prop Firms (Full Walkthrough)

## Introduction: The Power of the 1-Minute Timeframe
Waqar explains that the 1-minute (1m) timeframe is often feared by traders due to "noise." However, he argues that for scalping, the 1m timeframe provides the most precise entries and the highest risk-to-reward (RR) ratios. This strategy is specifically designed for passing **Prop Firm Challenges** by targeting quick, high-probability moves.

## The Strategy Core: Rules-Based Scalping
The strategy is built on a series of strict rules to remove emotion and guesswork. It focuses on identifying specific setups that repeat daily in the markets.

### 1. Market Context & Bias
*   **Higher Timeframe (HTF) Alignment**: Always start with the 15-minute (15m) or 1-hour (1h) timeframe to determine the daily bias (Bullish or Bearish).
*   **The Pullback**: Look for a pullback into a valid HTF Point of Interest (POI), such as an Order Block or Fair Value Gaps (FVG).

### 2. The Setup: "VWAP Flip" & "Offsides Reversal"
Waqar introduces two primary setups for 1m scalping:
*   **The VWAP Flip**: Using the Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP) as a dynamic level of support/resistance. When price "flips" over the VWAP after a liquidity sweep, it confirms institutional momentum.
*   **The Offsides Reversal**: Identifying where "breakout" traders are trapped (going "offsides") and then trading in the opposite direction once their stops are hit.

## Step-by-Step Execution (The Blueprint)

### Step 1: Identify Liquidity Sweeps (1m)
*   Wait for price to sweep a recent 1m high or low within an HTF POI.
*   This sweep "clears the board" of retail stops.

### Step 2: Market Structure Break (MSB)
*   After the sweep, wait for a strong, impulsive move that breaks the recent 1m structure in the opposite direction.
*   The break must be "clean" (large, energetic candles).

### Step 3: Entry at the "Premium/Discount" Level
*   Use the Fibonacci Retracement tool from the start of the impulsive move to its end.
*   **For Longs**: Only enter in the **Discount** zone (below 50%).
*   **For Shorts**: Only enter in the **Premium** zone (above 50%).
*   Look for an unmitigated 1m Order Block or FVG within these zones for the exact entry.

## Risk Management & Trade Management
*   **Stop Loss (SL)**: Placed just above/below the high/low of the sweep.
*   **Take Profit (TP)**: 
    *   **TP1**: Recent structural high/low (for a quick 2:1 or 3:1 RR).
    *   **TP2**: The next major HTF liquidity pool.
*   **Prop Firm Rule**: Never risk more than 0.5% to 1% per trade to protect the drawdown limits.

## The "Holy Trinity" Applied to 1m
1.  **Inducement**: The 1m trap before the sweep.
2.  **Time**: Only trade during the **London Open** (2:00 AM - 5:00 AM EST) and **New York Open** (8:00 AM - 11:00 AM EST).
3.  **LTC (Lower Timeframe Confirmation)**: The 1m MSB itself is the confirmation for the HTF move.

## Key Mindset Shifts for Scalpers
*   **Don't Overtrade**: There may only be 1-2 high-quality setups per session.
*   **Accept Small Losses**: Scalping involves high frequency; small losses are part of the "cost of doing business."
*   **Patience is Profit**: Waiting for the *exact* criteria to be met is what separates a professional scalper from a gambler.

## Conclusion
Scalping on the 1m timeframe is not about gambling; it's about being a "sniper." By combining HTF bias with 1m liquidity sweeps and disciplined entry rules, traders can consistently grow prop firm accounts with high precision.
